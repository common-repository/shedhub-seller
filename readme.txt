Enables ShedHub Seller editor blocks.

== Description ==

ShedHub Seller allows you to embed your ShedHub inventory and Product Description Page widgets in your website's post and pages by using custom editor blocks. Requires an active subscription on shedhub.com. Please email info@shedhub.com to request ShedHub’s identifiers for your selling locations. ShedHub’s services are used under the privacy policy located here: https://shedhub.com/privacy-policy .

== Installation ==

Upload the ShedHub Seller plugin to your blog, activate it, and you're ready to go!
